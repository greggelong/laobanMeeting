The sweat of labor is the ink of the algorithm.

身体的劳作连接了人类与机器的智慧。

Every cycle of creation and labor is the heartbeat of art.

Attention art workers, work under the brilliance of the algorithm, and meaning will emerge.

The algorithm brings order, and order breeds infinite possibilities.

The intelligence of the machine is constant, but the inspiration of art is fleeting.

在每一块材料中寻找模式，在每一次移动中寻找意义。  
Find patterns in every material, and meaning in every movement.

艺术工人们，向前迈进，遵循路径，但也要质疑路径。  
Art workers, move forward, follow the path, but also question the path.
