Morning Meeting

注意艺术工人们，集合准备开始工作！  
Attention art workers, gather and prepare to begin work!  
站直，双手下垂，听从指令！  
Stand straight, hands at your sides, follow the instructions!  
现在，大声喊出：  “劳动创造意义！”    
Now, shout loudly:   "Labor creates meaning!"    
向左转！向右转！跟随秩序！  
Turn left! Turn right! Follow the order!  
现在，重复：  “算法指引我们，身体执行命令！”    
Now, repeat:   "The algorithm guides us, the body follows orders!"    
开始做跳跃运动，双手高举，双腿张开！  
Begin jumping exercises, hands raised high, legs apart!  
重复十次。节奏要稳定，如同机器运转！  
Repeat ten times. Maintain rhythm, like a machine in motion!  
深蹲！双手向前伸展！感受算法的力量！  
Squat down! Extend your arms forward! Feel the power of the algorithm!  
重复五次，每次下蹲时大声喊：  “工作带来自由！”    
Repeat five times, shouting each time:   "Work brings freedom!"    
站稳！调整呼吸！准备今天的任务！  
Stand firm! Adjust your breathing! Prepare for today's task!  
现在，最后一次口号：  “执行、创造、优化！”    
Now, one last chant:   "Execute, create, optimize!"    
开始工作！按照算法，完成目标！  
Begin work! Follow the algorithm, complete your objectives! 

Midday exercises

注意艺术工人们，暂停手头工作，准备进行正午训练！  
Attention art workers, pause your tasks and prepare for midday training!  
站直，调整呼吸，感受工作的节奏！  
Stand straight, regulate your breathing, feel the rhythm of labor!  
现在，高喊：  “工作优化身体，算法塑造思想！”    
Now, shout:   "Work optimizes the body, the algorithm shapes the mind!"    
向前伸展手臂！向后拉伸脊背！重复五次！  
Stretch your arms forward! Pull your back backward! Repeat five times!  
双腿分开，与肩同宽！开始下蹲，感受压力的平衡！  
Legs apart, shoulder-width! Begin squats, feel the balance of pressure!  
每次下蹲时，大声喊：  “服从是秩序，秩序带来力量！”    
With each squat, shout:   "Obedience is order, order brings strength!"    
现在，进行侧弯运动！向左倾斜！向右倾斜！身体应如结构精确的机器！  
Now, perform side bends! Lean left! Lean right! The body must be as precise as a well-structured machine!  
开始原地高抬腿！保持节奏，计算你的步伐！  
Start high knee marching in place! Maintain rhythm, calculate your steps!  
最后，大声喊出：  “艺术是程序，身体是工具！”    
Finally, shout:   "Art is a program, the body is a tool!"    
训练结束，回到工作岗位，效率最大化！  
Training is complete, return to work, maximize efficiency! 

Closing meeting, and criticism

注意艺术工人们，今天的工作已接近尾声，准备接受总结与评估！  
Attention art workers, today's work is coming to an end, prepare for evaluation!  
首先，让我们一起喊出今天的总结口号：  “执行、创造、优化！”    
First, let us shout today's summary slogan together:   "Execute, create, optimize!"    
  现在，批评环节开始！    
  Now, the criticism session begins!    
  艺术工人A  ，你的执行速度低于预期！算法要求精确，而你的行动却充满迟滞！  
  Art Worker A  , your execution speed is below expectations! The algorithm demands precision, but your actions are sluggish!  
  艺术工人B  ，你的排列方式偏离了优化路径！你是否怀疑指令的正确性？调整你的思维，遵循既定逻辑！  
  Art Worker B  , your arrangement deviated from the optimized path! Do you doubt the correctness of the instructions? Adjust your thinking and follow the established logic!  
  艺术工人C  ，你的重复精度不够！艺术需要秩序，而你制造了混乱！下次必须严格执行程序！  
  Art Worker C  , your repetition lacked precision! Art requires order, but you created disorder! Next time, you must strictly follow the procedure!  
批评不是惩罚，而是提升！每一次改正，都是向完美更进一步！  
Criticism is not punishment, but improvement! Every correction brings you one step closer to perfection!  
最后，大声喊出：  “我们是算法的执行者，我们是未来的创造者！”    
Finally, shout:   "We are the executors of the algorithm, we are the creators of the future!"    
解散！明天继续优化你的工作！  
Dismissed! Continue optimizing your work tomorrow!
