麻绳绷直，随山势下坠，张力之中显路径。
Stretch the hemp rope downhill with the mountain—tension reveals the path.
晨露尚未干，工具已映日光，动作先于命令。
Before the morning dew dries, tools catch the sun—motion precedes command.
流水边，一砖一石皆需对齐如经卷初展。
By flowing water, every brick and stone must align like an unfolding scroll.
锤声回响在竹林之巅，节奏引导结构。
The hammer echoes atop the bamboo grove—rhythm directs the frame.
钢材沿坡而上，回音验证其正直之形。
Steel climbs the slope—its echo confirms the upright form.
鸟飞不过画线之弧，绳墨已定天空之界。
No bird crosses the arched chalk line—plumb lines have mapped the sky.
湿泥托起石块，重量即允诺，位置即永恒。
Wet soil lifts stone—weight is promise, position is permanence.
草间工具闪耀如星辰，其序如天文历法。
Tools glint among grass like stars—their arrangement is astronomical.
碎砖归类，形状与用途共鸣于秩序之律。
Broken bricks sorted—their shapes and purpose resonate in ordered law.
钉子须朝向西北，顺风入木，如鹤落松林。
Nails must point northwest—driven with the wind like cranes into pines.
一线垂直，贯穿四方，竹影不敢偏移。
A single plumb line crosses all directions—bamboo shadows do not stray.
脚印深陷砂地，如古诗留痕，皆为未来之形。
Footprints sink into sand like verses—each mark shapes what will come.
